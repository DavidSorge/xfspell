Probe ordered into CheffalDattu firing Press Trust of India 13 The Tamil Nadu government has ordered judicial enquiry into the police firing on group of villagers who gathered at the sub-collectors office at Chengalpattu on October 10 in which one person was killed An official press release said here today that the chief minister Ms Jayalalitha had ordered judicial enquiry on the same day The government had also written to the Madras high court asking it to name judge to conduct the enquiry The incident was the fallout of land dispute There was an attempt to install statue of Mr Ambedkar. on the disputed land but the police seized the statue Agitated villagers who gathered at the revenue officials office turned violent leading to the police firing.
