Probe riots in Sambhal MP By Our Special Correspondent NEW DELHI March 31 Mr Ram Dhan prominent Janata party leader has demanded judicial into the communal riots at Sambhal Moradabad in which 13 people were killed He also wanted the UP government to take action against the officials for failure to visualise the deteriorating situation Mr Ram Dhan took the UP government to task for having failed for the second time in few months in preventing communal riots He said that the Sambhal incidents followed the Varanasi riots were grim reminder to the nation that the communal forces were still alive and active
