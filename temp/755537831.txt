15 arrested for Ahmedabad clash AHMEDABAD January 16 PTI Fifteen persons were arrested today in connection with last night's -clash between two groups in the Shahpur area of the city youth was killed when police opened fire to curb mob violence The situation in Shahpur and other affected areas of Kalupur and Dariapur remained peaceful today The indefinite curfew clamped on Shahpur since last night was continued during the day and night curfew would also remain in force, the police said
