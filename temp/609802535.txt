PM to tour riot areas By Staff Reporter BOMBAY May 20 The Prime Minister will tour the riot-torn areas of Bhiwandi Thane and Greater Bombay tomorrow according to the chief minister Mr Vasantrao Patil. Immediately after her arrival in the morning at Santa Cruz airport Mrs Gandhi will fly in helicopter to Bhiwandi Apart from meeting the victims families she will also receive members of the peace. committees After visiting the affected parts of the metropolis she will receive delegations at Raj Bhavan before returning to New Delhi at 630 pm
