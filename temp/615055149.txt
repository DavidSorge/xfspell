Curfew in Baroda area one dead BARODA August 19 UNI An indefinite curfew was clamped on the Wadi and the city police station areas here late tonight after violence erupted leaving one person dead several others wounded Hospital sources said that more than 30 people were admitted for treatment Most of them were wounded with sharp weapons while about six were hit by bullets they said The trouble began after shots were fired by the occupants of speeding cars in the Panigate locality, the police said
