Inquiry from IPCL blast NEW DELHI Nov 14 PTI The government has decided to set up seven-member high-powered committee of technical experts to inquire into the accident at IPCLs Maharashtra Gas Cracker Complex on November 5 claiming 20 lives The chairman of the enquiry committees will be Dr Mashelkar director National Chemical Laboratory Pune according to official sources The committee will enquire. into the causes of the accident It will also examine whether the safety systems and emergency procedure established for the complex were adequate and were refollowed
