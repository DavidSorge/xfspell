Tension prevails in Kashmir SRINAGAR August 16 PTI Scores of people including nearly 100 policemen were injured in repeated clashes between the police and ProPakistan demonstrators in the downtown localities for the second day today According to the deputy inspector general of police Kashmir range Mr Aivali about 100 policemen including superintendent of police were injured The police also opened fire at Malaratta and Bohrikadal to disperse the stone-throwing demonstrators who attacked police station at Khanyar and hurled petrol bombs at the police Mr Aivali said the demonstrators also made futile attempts to set fire to religious shrine in Ali Kadal area They also tried to loot shops in the adjoining Maharajgunj area
