32 hurt in clash over immersion By Our Correspondent September 16 Thirty two people were injured in clash between two communities at Dhaid about 30 km from here yesterday when procession taking the Ganesh image for immersion was passing through locality rights. All the injured have been admitted to the government hospital here Some home guards policemen and revenue officers were also injured in  incident On receiving the report of the incident the district collector Mr Ramanand Tiwari and the superintendent of police Mr Promod Bisariya rushed to Dhad with reinforcements
