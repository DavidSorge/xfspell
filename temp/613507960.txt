Poor response to Ahmedabad bandh AHMEDABAD Feb 9 UNI The Ahmedabad bandh called by the Shiv Sena to protest against failure of the police to tackle the recent clashes in Bapu nagar evoked poor response today According to police sources life in the city remained unaffected with business establishments educational institutions and city bus services functioning normally Heavy police were made in the walled city and other areas as precautionary measure the police said Group clashes broke out in the suburb of Bapunagar on January 30 during victory procession.
