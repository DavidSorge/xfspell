Stray Incidents Three stray incidents of knife attack were reported on the morning and afternoon of Thursday from parts of Central Bombay 12hour curfew was imposed in the evening No incidents were however reported from any part of the city between 3-30 pm and midnight Continued police drive against suspected anti-social elements resulted in the arrest of 81 men bringing the total number of persons taken into custody during the last four days to 425 Of these 26 had been apprehended carrying lethal weapons The curfew was effective from 6 pm on Thursday to 6 am on Friday covered areas. bounded by Babula Tank Road Chinchbunder Road Dr Maheshwart Road Sandhurst Road and Ibrahim Rahim Road
