30 militants killed in Lanka COLOMBO January 19 UNIDPA At least 30 Tamil militants were killed in gunbattle with security forces early today in Sri Lanka's major eastern coastal city of Batticaloa defence ministry spokesman here who confirmed the incident said fighting was continuing at mid-day today major battles broke out when commandoes of the special task force were called in following dawn ambush in which guerillas in battle strength killed two policemen and wounded two other Helicopters fired on guerillas from above according to residents contacted by telephone Informed sources also said civilians were caught in crossfire and probably killed The defence ministry spokesman said 13 militants were killed in gunfire and six others committed suicide by consuming cyanide Ahmedabad curfew AHMEDABAD January 19 PTI Night curfew will only be for nine hours from tomorrow as complete peace prevailed in the city today, the police said
