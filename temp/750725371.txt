Ahmedabad clashes lead to curfew The Times of India News Service AHMEDABAD January 4 Indefinite curfew has been imposed on Dariapur and Kalupur the communally-sensitive areas in the walled city. following sudden spurt of violence which erupted late this evening At least 18 people suffered knife or bullet wounds and in stone-throwing the condition of six are said to be serious The trouble reportedly began over kite-flying However heavy stop-nethrowing began soon in both Dariapur and Kalupur areas Three people were stabbed and two shops were also set afire The police after bursting Teargas shells opened fire Twenty-five rounds were reported to have been fired in which dozen people were injured Curfew was imposed as national measure
