Peace holds in Ahmedabad AHMEDABAD July 31 With the situation returning to near normalcy in the textile city the police continued their drive against antisocial elements Four more persons were detained under the NSA today taking the total number of arrests to 41 the city additional commissioner of police Mr Kami Pillai said He said last night police officiated unearthed mini-arms factory in Asarwa area and arrested two persons under the anti-terrorist Act and Arms Act.
