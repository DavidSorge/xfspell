Curfew extended in Birlagram INDORE December 22 The district magistrate of Ujjain today extended the curfew by another 24 hours in the twin townships of Nagda and Birlagram which witnessed large-scale violence and police firing yesterday The one hour recess in the curfew between 3 pm and 4 pm passed off quietly and the authorities described the situation as completely peaceful and fully under. The authorities confirmed that 38 people including 30 police personnel were injured in the disturbances Four of the injured policemen were reported to be in serious condition
