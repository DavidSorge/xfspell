Curfew clamped on Belgaum The Times of India News Service BbLGAUM June 17 Fitty-two sectors including Shahapur police station limits of Belgaum city have been brought under 33 hours curfew from 11 pm last night This step was taken in the wake of incidents of arson and looting of shops and public properties in the last two days About 15 platoons of the state and reserve police have been deployed in The sensitive areas of the city coming under the curfew The situation is reported to be tense but under control The lingering sporadic incident following the death of two people due to stabbing few days ago has sparked off further incidents after the burial of one of the deceased Autorickshaw drivers have decided to go off the road till normalcy is restored.
