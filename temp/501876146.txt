Curfew clamped on Alterah LUCKNOW October 5 UNI curfew has been imposed in Aligarh following violent clashes between two sections of people At least six persons were reported to have been and several injured in clashes this afternoon according to preliminary official reports received here late in the evening While details are awaited heavy additional police and PAC forces have been rushed to Aligarh Some cases of arson were also reported The reports said violence erupted in the town following the death today of a person who had been stabbed on Tuesday night The situation was said to be under control following effective police action The district magistrate was among those injured in the heavy between the two crowds The police opened fire to bring the situation under control Details of the firing were not immediately known
