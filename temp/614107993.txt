Two killed in TN clash MADURAI March 24 UNI Two persons were stabbed to death and 27 injured in clash between two communities in the Dindigul town in Quide-e-Millet district of Tamil Nadu today. According to reports received here the Hindu Munnani was to take out procession in the town to protest against the naming of the district after the Muslim leader meanwhile good number of Muslims had gathered in front of mosque at Begampur to prevent the procession Stabbing incidents followed in which two persons were killed and 27 were injured The bodies of the two persons were brought to Dindigul Government Hospital where the injured were also admitted.
