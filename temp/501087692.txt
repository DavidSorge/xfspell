Report by Janata MPs NEW DELHI April 16 PTI three-member team of Janata MPs is understood to have blamed group owing allegiance to an erstwhile Cong leader of Bihar so-called cultural organisation faction of the Congress for the pre-planned communal riots in Jamshedpur the steel town of Bihar The team comprising of Mr Madhu Lim aye Dt Sarojani Mahishi and Mr Ghulam Ahmad Khan visited the steel town on Saturday and submitted its report to the party chief Mr Chandra Shekhar here today.
