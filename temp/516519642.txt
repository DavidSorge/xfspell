Bangalore mob teargassed BANGALORE September 5 PTI The police today burst several teargas shells to disperse2000-strong stonethrowing crowd at Viveknagar here in the scene of yesterday's violence The trouble started when the body of yesterday's police firing victim was brought in procession to the spot where temporary place of worship erected by minority community was demolished by the Bangalore Development Authority On reaching the spot section of the crowd turned violent and threw stones at the police The police said when lathi-charge proved ineffective they burst teargas shells.
