20 arrested during Gujarat bandh AHMEDABAD total of 20 persons have been arrested in the city for interiors during the 24-hour Gujarat bandh called by the Vishwa Hindu Parishad VHP on Monday which evoked mixed response and sparked off incidents of stone-throwing and arson Police said three buses were partially burnt down and five others stoned in different parts of the city While most shops commercial establishments remained closed offices banks school and colleges functioned normally in most parts of the city Vehicular traffic was normal but there was less congestion on the roads nearly 20 students tore up the answer papers of 200 students at an examination centre at Navgujarat Commerce College where the police said school and bank was forcibly closed at two other places in the city Police said the overall law and order situation was under control in the city and additional forces had been deployed in all the sensitive areas to keep vigil put.
