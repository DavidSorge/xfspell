Khamgaon Quiet The Times of India News Servk NAGPUR September 16 Khamgaon continued to be quiet today The Divisional Commissioner of Nagpur Mr Qamruddin visited Malkapur and Khamgaon and inspected the relief work being carried on there The damage to property in the disturbances in Khamgaon on Thursday is estimated at Rs 11000 Financial assistance amounting to Rs 1200 has been given to the sufferers so far Mr Qamruddin appealed to the people of Khamgaon and Malkapur to maintain peace and amity.
