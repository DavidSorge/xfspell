1 KILLED 6 INJURED Rival Groups Clash RAJKOT April 19 One person was killed and six received injuries in clash between the rival groups in the town yesterday afternoon The clash it was stated was sequel to the marriage of Rajput girl to man who belonged to the Aher community According to the police some persons entered the girls house yesterday afternoon and started assaulting the inmates Lathies and knives were freely used in the fight in which six persons sustained injuries One of the injured later to his injuries in hospital The condition of one more reported to be serious PTI
