Sethi to visit riot -Mt areas NEW DELHI May 19 PTI Perturbed over the continued rioting in Bombay and Bhiwandi and the violence spreading to other areas in Maharashtra Prime Minister Mrs Indira Gandhi tonight asked the home minister Mr Sethi to rush to the riot-affected areas for an on-the-spot study Mr Sethi left for Bombay by the Indian Airlines night flight From Bombay Mr Sethi will leave by helicopter for Bhiwandi tomorrow morning The Prime Minister has also expressed her deep concern at the evidence and group clashes in those places deputation of Jamiat Ulama-iHind today urged Mr Sethi to take strong action against the Shiv Sena for inciting the riots Our staff reporter adds Mr Sethi arrived here on Saturday night and is expected to make an aerial survey of Bhiwandi at 630 am tomorrow
