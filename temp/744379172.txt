12 KILLED IN BIHAR BORDER RIOTS POLICE FIRING Bihar July 22 Twelve persons were killed in rioting and police firing this morning Several people were injured in the wake of murderous mob attacks and arson at the village of Kanki in Chakalia thana in Islampur sub-division on the Bihar-West Bengal border it is officially learnt here Official sources said that the trouble started yesterday. evening when the Marxist Communist Party workers sentenced person called Motilal to pay penalty of Rs 500 in peoples court When the Forward Block intervened two of their leaders and some others were beaten up allegedly at the instance of CPI leader -PTI
