Situation peaceful in Hyderabad HYDERABAD July 26 PTI The situation in the entire Old City and adjoining areas remained peaceful and incident-free and the curfew would be lifted from 6 am to 8 pm. tomorrow The atmosphere in the four police station limits where curfew was lifted this morning and in the other areas where it was relaxed for four hours was tension-free the city police commissioner. Mr Rama Rao told reporters today Indefinite curfew was clamped following incidents of violence on the last day of the Mahankali Jatra on Monday in which two persons lost their lives.
