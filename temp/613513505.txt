Sena leaders ban extended The Times of India News Service AURANGABAD May 30 The district authorities today extended the ban on the entry of the four Shiv Sena leaders Mr Chaggan Bhujbal Mr Manohar Joshi Mr Sudhir Joshi and Mr Madhukar Sarpottar by fortnight The ban order on the Shiv Sena leaders was issued on May 20 at the instance of the state government to prevent them from giving provocative speeches in the riot-hit towns of Aurangabad and Paithan As the earlier order expired today it was extended till June 15 Meanwhile one more independent corporator Mr Moreshwar Save has filed civil suit in the Aurangabad bench of the Bombay high court praying for ban on the general body meeting of the Aurangabad municipal corporation scheduled to be held on June 3.
