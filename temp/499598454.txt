Delhi area calm as curfew is By Our Staff Correspondent NEW DELHI September 17 There was no tension in the Jama Masjid and adjoining areas today as curfew was relaxed from 6 am to 6 pm The curfew will be lifted at 6 am tomorrow and the situation will be reviewed in the afternoon According to the police there was no untoward incident People came out of their houses for their daily shopping mingled freely in the area which was the scene of violent clash between two groups last Friday night.
