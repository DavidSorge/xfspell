Curfew continues in Rajouri JAMMU August 24 PTI The indefinite curfew clamped in the border town of Rajouri continued for the second day today after night-long clashes between two groups of people The Overall situation in the town 177 km from here was officially described as well under control and peaceful The border township of Rajouri was brought under curfew yesterday when two groups indulged in large-scale violence using sharpedged weapons and country-made pistols in which several persons were injured including two with bullet wounds
