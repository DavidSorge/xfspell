14 hurt in riot near Ratnagiri By Staff Reporter BOMBAY July 14 Fourteen people were injured in stonethrowing and eight houses were burnt in clash between two communities at Dabhol in Ratnagiri district yesterday Police sources said the trouble started when one boy fired at another boy and injured him over previous The police first resorted to lathi-charge and then fired three rounds in the air No one was injured in the police firing.
