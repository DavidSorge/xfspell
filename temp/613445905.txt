Shoot-at-sight in Balasore BHUBANESWAR March 28 UNI Curfew was imposed and shoot-at-sight orders issued to the police in Soro in Balasore district after two people were injured in police firing violent mob during the BJP-sponsored Orissa bandh today Official sources said the mob attacked CRPF patrol with petrol bombs and set afire several vehicles and some houses during the bandh called protest against the governments failure to protect life and property during the Bhadrak riots on Sunday The sources said Bhadrak town however remained peaceful with an indefinite curfew being in force The CRPF staged flag march in the town today Life in the state capital remained normal barring some stray incidents of stone throwing at buses Vehicles plied and offices and educational institutions. functioned as usual Train services were also unaffected.
