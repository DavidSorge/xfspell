TENSION EASES Door-to-door Visits By Bihar Ministers PATNA March 22 The situation in Jamshedpur had improved consisting Mr Ram Lakhan Singh Yadav Bihars PWD Minister here today No fresh incidents had been reported The Chief Minister Mr Sahay and the Education Minister Mr Sinha were making door-to-door visits in the affected localities which had salutary effect in easing the tension Mr Yadav said Military detachments have taken their positions at strategic points to guard the factory and the plants purely as precautionary measure Three rescue camps have been set up at Jamshedpur for the safety of the minority community Armed police pickets have been reinforced in different areas Over 600 persons have been taken into police custody
