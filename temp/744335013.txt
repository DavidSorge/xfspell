Fresh clashes in Aurangabad The Times of India News Service AURANGABAD January 18 Curfew was on this today afternoon following fresh incidents of arson and violence There was relative calm in the morning when the dusk-lo-dawn curfew imposed yesterday was lifted with most of the people preferring to stay indoors However in the afternoon rival groups came on to the streets and started pelting. stones on each other The police made lathi-charge lo disperse the crowds Thirty rioters were taken into custody The trouble began yesterday when procession organized by the Shiv Sena demanding common civil code was attacked near place of worship Meanwhile the situation in Sangamner in Ahmednagar district which was also rocked by riots yesterday was said to be under control today The police said no fresh incidents were reported.
