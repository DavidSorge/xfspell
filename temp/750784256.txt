1 killed in Baroda police firing BARODA Sept 30 PTI One person was killed and two others were injured one of them seriously when the police fired four rounds to disperse stone-throwing warring groups in Brahman Falia in the Fetehpura area of the city late last night police said Trouble began when some persons threw stones at the rival group as they were playing garba in the area, the police said adding that firing was resorted to after bursting of tear gas shells proved ineffective in quelling the groups An indefinite curfew was imposed since this morning in Brahman Falia Fatehpura Navawad and Yetpura areas falling under the city police station The injured were admitted to the SSG hospital here.
