Agra Firozabad Ban AGRA October 4 The Officiating District Magistrate Mr Seth today promulgated prohibitory order under Section 144 Cr PC in Agra and Firozabad for a period of one month with immediate effect The order is precautionary measure following the Aligarh disturbances According to press release the order bans processions and any form of writing or speech calculated to cause breach of the peace The order also prohibits the carrying of lethal weapons and the collecting of brick-bats and explosives INS.
