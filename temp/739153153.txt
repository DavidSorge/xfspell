Alleged assailant held in Hyderabad HYDERABAD June 6 The alleged assailant said to be responsible for stabbing to death the owner of cycle taxi shop in the city last week and who had been absconding. was arrested today The stabbing incident had led to disturbances in the city Twenty-nine more persons have been taken into custody by the police bringing the total number of detentions to 339 Peace. prevailed in the city today No incidents were reported Police patrolling in the affected areas and vulnerable points continues The Commissioner of Police today issued orders prohibiting the Assembly of five or more persons and carrying of weapons and collection of stones and other missiles for another week beginning from tomorrow PTI
