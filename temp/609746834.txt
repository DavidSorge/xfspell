Indefinite curfew in Hyderabad Times of India Never Service HYDERABAD July 23 Indefinite curfew was clamped on the old city and southern parts of Hyderabad from 6 pm today following violent clashes between groups of people Tension gripped the area yesterday when youth was stabbed to death and two others sustained knife injuries during the Bonalu procession to mark the Mahankai festival The police commissioner said despite elaborate police bandobust there were stray clashes today The situation was under control and curfew was imposed to measure he added About yesterdays stabbing incident the authorities said the trouble was confined to one area which was quickly isolated Rumours were being spread and there were exaggerated versions of the trouble The police have appealed to the people not give credence to such rumours.
