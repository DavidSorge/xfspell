Peace returns to Gujarat The Times of India News Service AHMEDABAD December 12 There was remarkable improvement in riot-hit cities and towns in Gujarat today with no single untoward incident reported from any part of the state till this evening While curfew was completely lifted from Bhavnagar and Palitana the curfew relaxation granted in phased manner in 12 cities and towns including Baroda. Jambusar Sidhpur Palanpour Mehsana Anand Dhandhuka Junagadh and Jamnagar during the day passed off peacefully The chief minister Mr Chimanbhai Patel and the home minister Mr Patel claimed that the situation was fast returning to normal They said that no single incident of violence had so far been reported from Surat city which was the focal point of violence in the entire state
