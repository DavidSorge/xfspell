Lurfew eased in Pune PUNE February 21 PTI The night curfew was relaxed for two hours here today for the first time in several days dose on the heels of measure at Solapur following the return of near normalcy in the violence affected cities of Maharashtra the police reported tonight In Pandharpur also where curfew was clamped last night following conflagration the situation was stated to be completely normal though curfew was enforced for the second night tonight for reduced span of time as precautionary measure The damage to property in the outbreak of violence in Pune since February 13 was estimated at Rs lakhs the police reported on the basis of complaints The police arrested 38 people in connection with the violence and eight cases were registered against them while 51 others were taken into custody as precautionary measure the district superintendent of police rural Mr Surudkar said
