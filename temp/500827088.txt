Another fatal stabbing in Sambhal town MORADABAD April 2 One more person was stabbed to death and another sustained serious injuries when the curfew was relaxed for one hour today in the riot-affected Sanitown near here The district magistrate Mr Farahat Ali told newsmen that curfew had been reimposed soon after the incident for an indefinite period He said the death-roll had reached 16 as result of today's casualty In all 22 persons and 130 had been taken into custody by the police since March 29 when she riot started on the town he added Samachar
