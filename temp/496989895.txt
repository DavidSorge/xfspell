Curfew relaxed in Allahabad The Times of India News Service ALLAHABAD February 24 With no fresh incidents reported from any area today curfew was relaxed in the city from 7-30 am to 6-30 pm however be reimposed from 6-30 pm to 7-30 am tomorrow The city was fast returning to normal following appeals from all political parties and prominent citizens for the maintenance of peace and communal harmony Some more arrests were made today in connection with the violent clash between two groups of persons and the firing on Monday.
