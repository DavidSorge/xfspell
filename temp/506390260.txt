Violence continues in TN 1 killed The Times of India News Service MADRAS October 1 1 In fallout of yesterday's killing of the Hindu Munnani leader Mr Rajagopal street violence broke out in which one person was stabbed to death and incidents of stone-throwing and bus-burning continued for the second day in many parts of Tamil Nadu The PCC president Mr Vazhapadi Ramamurthy alleged the police had failed to handle the situation.
