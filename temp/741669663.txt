2 killed 21 injured in group clash MATHURA At least two persons were killed and 21 injured in communal clashes between the members of two communities here on Wednesday The clashes followed stabbing of youth by notorious criminal belonging to another community on Tuesday Another youth who was hit by bullet in cross-fire between the members of the two communities succumbed to his injuries on Wednesday officials said an earlier report had said that police opened fire and burst tear-gas shells to disperse the warring groups However principal secretary home HC Gupta denied in Lucknow that the police had opened fire or that anybody had been killed in police firing PTI
