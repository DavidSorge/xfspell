No day curfew in Kalyan today by Stan Reporter August 13 Free movement will be allowed to citizens Kalyan from 6 am to 9 pm tomorrow in view of the improved situation in the town today the collector District magistrate Mr Anil Gokak announced here on Wednesday He said there was no untoward incident reported today The curfew was relaxed from 6 am to 12 noon and from 3 pm to 6 pm Mr Gokak has appealed to the school and college authorities to reopen their institutions from tomorrow Over 1000 Muslims offered ID prayers at the historic Durgadi fort between 815 am and 845 am Muslims in other parts of the town also offered prayers
