Processions to collect aid for riot-hit By Staff Reporter BOMBAY June 2 Different organised processions in various parts of the city and suburbs to collect donations for the recent riot victims The Malabar Hill Citizens Forum will take out procession which will begin from Haji Ali at 830 am and terminate at Raj Bhavan where the donations in kind will be handed over to Mrs Bilke's Latif The cash donations will be presented to the chief minister Mr Vasantrao Patil at the Varsha number of leading film artists and other prominent citizens will participate in the procession through the not affected areas of Thane city has been organised on behalf of the Thane Law College peace committee It will begin at 9 am Andheri Jaycees youth club has planned peace march and relief assistance campaign It will start from the Andheri railway station
