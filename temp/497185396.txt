Palanpur quiet The Times of India New Service AHMED ABAD October 18 The situation in Palanpur district headquarters of Banaskantha was quiet and well under control today after incidents of rioting and arson on Tuesday evening according to reports reaching the state police headquarters here However as precautionary measures the district collector has re-imposed curfew in the town from 6 pm to 8 am Six persons injured in police firing and 22 others injured in stone-throwing were reported to be progressing in the local hospital The district collector has banned the carrying of weapons assembly of more than four people in the town for 15 days The police have been posted at almost all street corners and heavy patrolling is continuing in the town.
