Two die in Adoni clash over Dasara By Our Special Correspondent HYDERABAD October 12 One person died when the police opened fire to disperse an unruly mob and another was stabbed to death in clashes between two groups in Adoni in Kufnool district yesterday evening Trouble arose over the issue of taking out Dasara procession via mosque Curfew clamped on the area immediately brought the situation. under control Became a communal trouble last year in Adoni the district collector and the superintendent of police as precautionary measure had been camping in the town for the past few days to ensure that the Dasara procession to be taken out yesterday would be peaceful.
