Tulaba chief arrested by Our Staff Correspondent SRINAGAR August 22 THE controversial international Islamic youth conference convened by the Jamiat-i-Tuhba which was to have begun it three-day deliberations here today proved non-starter The day began with the arrest of the Tulaba chairman Sheikh Tajumal Islam Tulaba activities however registered their protest against the governments action by courting arrest in batches at Lai Chowk the chief business centre of the city According to Tulaba sources at least 150 workers were arrested The inspector-general of police Mr Peer Ghulam Hassanshah claimed on the other hand that the Tulaba activists were picked up from various points between La Chowk and Regal Chowk during the day He put the figure of arrests in Srinagar at 26.
