Jogeshwari normal By Staff Reporter BOMBAY February 4 Today the eighth day after communal riot rocked the suburb of Jogeshwari for the second time in month the situation continued to be normal Police said there were no untoward incidents during the period when curfew had been relaxed from 6 am to 7 pm today As further precautionary measure curfew has been reimposed from 7 pm tonight to 6 am tomorrow Meanwhile Dr Ishaq Jamkhanwala former minister has blamed the lower ranks of the police for revealing communal outlook in the recent riot in Jogeswari He has called upon the police to protect the lives and property of citizens during the riots
