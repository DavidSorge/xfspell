Discourage Rumours BOMBAY PUBLIC URGED TO BE CALM The Government of Bombay today appealed to every citizen of the State to discourage rumours and hand over rumour-mongers and mischievous persons The police press note said some mischievous elements in Bombay city availing themselves of the disturbances in Calcutta have set afoot number of rumours of various types and are making an effort to give currency to These rumours are false and there is perfect peace in the city Government trust that the public will not put any faith in any of these rumours and will fight rumour mongers Government desire to remind every citizen that it Is his or her duty to discourage such rumours to contradict them and hand over rumourmongers and mischievous persons to the police
