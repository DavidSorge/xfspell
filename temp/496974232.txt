Moradabad curfew extended The Times of India News Service MORADABAD March 6 The curfew imposed last night in the wake of clashes between two groups of people has been extended to 5 am tomorrow. It was relaxed for some time today One person was killed and 17 were injured in yesterday's clashes The trouble followed heated argument at polling booth over the identity of woman voter Polling was however affected The authorities are rounding up bad characters Thirty people were arrested this morning for violation Of the curfew Two PAC battalions are patrolling
