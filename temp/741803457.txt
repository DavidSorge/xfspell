West Delhi calm but tense NEW DELHI June 29 PTI The Lt Governor Mr Romesh Bhandari today announced an ex-gratia payment of Rs 20000 to the next of kin of Ranjit Singh who was killed in the riots broke out last night in the Tilak Vihar area of West Delhi Meanwhile the case of rioting in which about 11 persons including two police officers were injured has been transferred to the crime branch. for investigation into the allegation that these riots were planned Denying that the riots were planned senior police officer said that situation in the area was calm but tense but police were keeping close watch on the situation.
