Curfew on Beed town The Times of India News Service AURANGABAD January 12 An indefinite curfew has been clamped in Beed town of Marathwada region since this afternoon where communal clashes broke out. between two communities Four persons were stabbed The trouble started when the students of the Akhil Bharatiya Vidyarthi Parishad ABVP while observing Vivekanand Jayanti displayed the photograph of Swami Vivekanand at one side and picture of the broken Babri Masjid on other They had also displayed provocative placards in the college premises An irate mob attacked the ABVP activists injured at least four students Heavy police have been ordered to prevent the riots from spreading to other districts
