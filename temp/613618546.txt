Onus of riots lie with Cone to NAGPUR May 26 PTI The president of the Maharashtra pradesh congress committee and former MP Mr Dhabe said the Congress was equally responsible for instigating the recent communal riots in Aurangabad Reacting to reported statement by the Maharashtra chief minister Mr Chavan blaming the Shiv Sena for the group clashes he said both the Shiv Sena and the Congress. are tarred with the same brush He told that banning the Shiv Sena for its communal stand would only aggravate the problem by giving it undeserved publicity criticising the government for diluting the concept of socialism in the country he said that the government is encouraging privatisation of the public sector instead of improving the standard of the public-sector He also announced that the Congress would support the Jan Morcha leader Mr Singh in the Allok Sabha by-election
