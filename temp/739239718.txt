Police Open Fire CALICUT RIOT Eight Persons Injured The Times of India News Service CALICUT March 28 Eight men were injured this evening when the police opened fire on defiant mob which attacked them in front of mosque at Naduvaltam three miles iron here contingent of armed Malabar Special Police was rushed to the scene as large crowd gathered to prevent procession accompanied by music passing in front of the mosque The District Magistrate Malabar who reached the spot accompanied by the District Suof Police ordered lathi-charge when the crowd threw stones at the police and refused. to disperse The mob then grew unruly attacked them with iron bars Some policemen were injured and helmet or constable was smashed The police then opened fire The injured persons were taken to hospital
