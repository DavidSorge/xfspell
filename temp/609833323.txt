Curfew lifted in Hyderabad areas The Times of India News Service HYDERABAD August 5 In view of the peaceful situation in the city yesterday day curfew was lifted from six more police stations. including Habibnagar and Asifnagar However partial curfew during night will continue in 13 police station areas 1 Another BJP leader Mr Baddam Balreddy was arrested under the National Security Act taking the total NSA arrests to eight Meanwhile an AICC team arrived here yesterday to study the communal situation.
