Three killed in Gujarat - Riots The Times of India News Service AHMEDABAD Jan 16 Three persons including woman were killed and six were injured in police firing and stabbing in Broach town as violence broke out between two groups last night The police also opened fire and burst teargas shells in Anand and Kalol to quell the rioting mob Barring stray incidents Ahmedabad yesterday remained comparatively peaceful The trouble was sparked off in the curfew-bound Broach town after 300-strong mob went to neighbouring locality and had heated exchange with the residents where two groups clashed in Anand town yesterday when 300-strong mob attacked place of worship in the Kasba area
