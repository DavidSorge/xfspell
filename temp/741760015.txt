REPORTS FROM STATES Mysore Probe Into Police Firing Likely BAGALKOT The Times of India News Service November 3 1 The Mysore Government is likely to order an impartial inquiry into the police firing following communal disturbances at Bagalkot on October 26 One person died and several others were in the ruling Answering series of short notice question by Mr Patil and Mr Mohsin both Congress and Mr Narasimhan Communist Chief Minister Mr Jatti said in the State Atoday that proposal to institute an impartial inquiry into the Bagalkot incident was under consideration of the Government Mr Jatti also gave an account of the loss of property suffered at Bagalkot during the encounter between workers and some Muslims The Chief Minister said that peace committee consisting of the Sub-Divisional Magistrate as chairman and leading citizens had been formed at Bagalkot.
