No incidents Biharsharif NEW DELHI May 9 PT0 Peace has settled on Biharsharif the headquarters of Nalanda district in Bihar rocked by communal violence stemming from trivial incident on April 30. According to the chief minister Dr Jagannath Mishra The situation in Biharsharif continues to be peaceful since the last three days There has been no communal incident in the entire of Nalanda Dr Mishra said in statement at the airport here today before flying back to Patna after brief visit Dr Mishra arrived here this morning following snap visit by the Union minister of state for railways Mr Jaffar Sharif to Patna and the troubled town Police and security forces mobilised with the help of the Central government are on round-the-clock duty he said.
