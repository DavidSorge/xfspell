Barabanki toll rises to 16 LUCKNOW May 6 PTI With one more person succumbing to injuries here last night the death toll in last Wednesday's firing incidents in Barabanki town near here has risen to 16 Fifteen of the dead were people taking part in demonstration and one was home guard official Meanwhile the situation continued to be peaceful in the town and no untoward incident has been reported. since May 1#
