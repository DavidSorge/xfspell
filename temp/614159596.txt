Curfew imposed in Aurangabad AURANGABAD September 21 PTI Curfew was imposed today in several areas of the city following the recurrent violence since Friday which has claimed one life of student and left at least 20 injured The curfew will be effective from 7 am today to 530 am tomorrow the district magistrate Mr Rajiv Sinha told newsmen The situation in the city was stated to be peaceful In all 225 persons have been arrested or detained so far in connection with the violence Patrolling had been intensified to prevent further trouble the police said The chief minister Mr Chavan announced an ex-gratia payment of Rs 10000 from the chief ministers relief fund to the km of 22-year-old Babasah Gulab Kamble last nights stab victim
