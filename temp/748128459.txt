Firing on rioters in Baroda AHMEDABAD July 28 The police fired eight rounds and burst tear-gas shells to quell two groups of people indulging in heavy stone throwing in the Vadi and Panigate police station areas of Baroda city today reports PTI No one was injured in the firing though one person was hurt in the stone-throwing police said In the city police station areas the police opened fire to disperse mob throwing stones on Tazias which were taken out in Bheswad locality this morning The police also fired six rounds to disperse two stone-throwing groups in the Panigate area Meanwhile. The situation in the riot-hit areas of Broach district Jambusar and Sojitra where curfew continued to remain in force was reported to be peaceful.
