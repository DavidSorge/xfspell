Prompt govt aid takes 4 days The Times of India News Service AHMEDABAD July 15 Though the government claims it has provided prompt assistance to the 5000 refugees in the six relief camps opened in the city to accommodate people who have fled from trouble-torn areas the fact is that these people have had to fend for themselves for four days Of the inmates about 3000 are women or children Government aid started coming in only yesterday and until then the refugees were looked after by people from the locality Even today there was no satisfactory medical aid in the four relief camps which this correspondent visited Private medicos are helping the inmates and the muhas been providing medicines only since yesterday.
