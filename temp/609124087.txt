Ahmed Bukhari 35 others held NEW DELHI Dec 9 The Naib Imam of Jama Masjid Ahmed Bukhari and 35 others were detained in the area today the police commissioner Mr Kaushal said her PTI reports He said while the situation in curfew-bound areas of the capital remained peaceful and under control the atmosphere at Jama masjid became tense around 3 pm when the Naib Imam issued all summoning people onto the roofs of their homes and raise slogans He said the police warned the assembled people not to come down on the road since it would amount to violation of curfew orders Fortunately there were no counter slogans from nearby areas and soon crack units of the police marched through the by-lanes of the crowded area to instil confidence in the people he said.
