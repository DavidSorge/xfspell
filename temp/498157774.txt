Curfew in Varanasi area VARANASI November 2 Curfew Tvas in the Chowk area from 10 am today following incidents of arson stabbing and Earlier parts of the city were placed under curfew following clash between two communities on October 23 According to the district magistrate Mr Das some miscreants and anti-social elements tried to set fire to religious places in the area resulting in some cases of stabbing and stone-throwing All business establishments including banks and educational institutions in the area have been closed Life in other parts of the city was normal Mr Das Samachar.
