Vidisha Inquiry Move BHOPAL August 7 The Madhya Pradesh Hindu Mahasabha has set up a four-man committee headed by Mr Basantrau Pradhan MLA to make an on-the-spot inquiry the recent incident in Vidisha where the police had to open fire to disperse an unruly mob of two communities The committee has been directed to submit its report to the President of the State Hindu Mahasabha Pandit Brindawandas Tewari Mr Tewari in a statement said that the situation in Vidisha was serious and appealed to the Hindu population of the town to maintain peace in the town PTI.
