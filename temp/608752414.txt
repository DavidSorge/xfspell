Five accused in Junta Masjid sectarian clash surrender By Our Crime Reporter MUMBAI Five persons allegedly involved in the sectarian clash near Junta Masjid on January 18 surrendered before the LT Mar police on Saturday evening It may be recalled that dispute over the circulation of handbill near the masjid had resulted in clash between the Deobandi and Barelvi sects killing two persons and leaving four others injured The names of the accused were given as Sayyed Mazhar Ali Hasan 38 Anees Zakeria Palwala 37 laves Mohammed Kharawala 35 AMI Dawood Mansoor 45 and Farookh Suleman Lakdawala 32
