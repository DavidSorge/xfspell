Imam of Delhi Masjid detained MODINAGAR March 5 PTI The police detained the Delhi Jama Masjid Imam Abdullah Bukhari along with 15 others including Canadian here today while they were on way to Meerut The Meerut district magistrate Mr Singh later banned their entry to the district. Those detained with the Imam belong to the Delhi Muslim League the police said Imam Bukhari who was detained at the Muradnagar guest house said the Babri Masjid dispute was being taken advantage of by goondas who are provoking violence Meerut peaceful LUCKNOW March 5 PTI With no untoward incident reported from Meerut during the past 24 hours the situation in the town was today officially stated to be completely peaceful.
