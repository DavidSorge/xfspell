Two killed in Bengal firms CALCUTTA November 9 PTI At least two persons were killed and six others were injured in group clash and subsequent police firing at Badarhat in West Bengal North 24Parganas district today according to an official report The sources said trouble started when large number of people generated the police station demanding the release of one person arrested in connection with the attack on places of worship near Harba on November 6 The mob turned violent and attacked the police station with brickbats and other missiles
