17 hurt in firing in Quilon TRIVANDRUM December 4 PTV The police fired in the air to disperse two clashing groups at Puthiakavu in Karunapally near Qulkn on Sunday night according to delayed reports reaching here The clash was sequel to dispute over the use of some land in front of place of worship When the police rushed to the spot and intervened the groups indulged in stonethrowing 17 persons including five policemen were injured toddy shop two sheds and stationery shop were set on fire during the disturbances Four persons were taken into custody in connection with the incidents Strong police pickets have posted in the area and prohibitory orders have been imposed.
