12 hurt in clashes near Amravati AMRAVATi Feb 18 PTI Twelve persons were injured five in police firing and seven in violent incidents when two groups clashed at village Kapus Talani about 70 km from Amravati late last night the police said here today The police opened fire to disperse the groups who used cycle chains crow bars and conduit wires in the clash Old enmity sparked off the trouble. Police said the mob also set afire seven shops and went on rampage which was brought under control when the police arrived on the scene Fire tenders from Amravati were summoned to the blaze. morning
