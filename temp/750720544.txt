1 dead in firing on revellers van staff Correspondent AHMEDABAD March 17 One man was killed when the SRP opened fire to disperse two warring groups of Holi revellers On the Sabarmati riverbed today Two SRP men were severely beaten up by the revellers who snatched away the rifle five cartridges of an SRP constable The rifle was later recovered but not the cartlie trouble reportedly started when some children playing in the knee-deep water of the river kicked up row over some trivial matter The quarreling children attracted their parents on either side of the river where there are two large slums Someone informed the SRP men camping behind the Hospital Three SRP personnel rushed to the spot but were dragged into the water and beaten up meanwhile another SRP constable who was little distance away from the commotion opened fire.
