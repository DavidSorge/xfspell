Curfew eased as city quiet By Staff Reporter BOMBAY December 13 No untoward incident was reported in the city today where daytime curfew was lifted in 1 5 sensitive areas Curfew would however continue to be enforced from 8 pm onwards till further orders The metropolis was calm and moving towards normalcy The police said The army will continue to patrol sensitive areas Official sources said that essential comments were being provided in riot-hit areas under police escort In Hospital 34 bodies on which post-mortem had been completed were still in the morgue as they had not been identified spokesperson for the hospital said that 1 1 7 bodies had been brought here for postmortem including 49 which had been claimed by relatives and 37 which were shifted to Cooper Hospital.
