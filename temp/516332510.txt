PM for Aligarh By Our Special Correspondent NEW DELHI November 22 The Prime Minister Mr Morarji Desai will be visiting Aligarh on Sunday Mr Desai will go round some of the disturbed areas and later. address public meeting Mr Desai will break his journey in Aligarh on his way back from Shantiniketan where earlier in the day he will address the annual convocation of Viswa Bharati University Aligarh quiet LUCKNOW November 22 PTI The riot-torn Aligarh town was quiet for the third consecutive day today official reports reaching here said However the curfew had been relaxed for eight hours ended 4 pm today was clamped again for the night as precautionary measure Meanwhile the police recovered 29 hand grenades and two guns from different areas of the town and arrested five people in this connection yesterday.
