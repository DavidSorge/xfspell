Police firing in Baroda BARODA September 29 PTI The police fired one round in the air to disperse unruly mobs indulging in stone-throwing in the Sultanpura area on the Lehripura New Road here evening No one was injured in the firing Except for this incident the overall situation in the city which was rocked by large-scale violence on Friday which left two killed and 10 injured was peaceful The police said they had no idea what they were doing.
