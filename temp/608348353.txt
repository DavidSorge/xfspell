Police-Mob Clash In UP One Killed Uttar Pradesh Sent 11 One person was killed and two others were injured when police party escort ing the Muharram in the village of Naithua in this district Thursday fired two rounds to disperse an unruly crowd armed with lethal weapons The crowd which obstructed the procession clashed with the police party injuring two constables with swords it later set fire to three houses and hurled stones and brickbats at the police The police earlier had made lathi charge to disperse the crowd Which however became more aggressive dusk-to-dawn curfew has been clamped down on the village by the district authorities for week The situation according to press note issued by the District Magistrate is now normal and the affected areas are being patrolled by members of village defence societies and armed concepts
