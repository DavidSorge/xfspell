1 killed 6 hurt in Gujarat clashes AHMEDABAD March 21 PTI One person was killed and six injured in group clashes at Nani Chirai village under Bhachau police station in Kutch district last night police said Police fired 14 rounds in the air to disperse the clashing groups after lathi-charge proved futile The incident followed an altercation between two groups at programme organized on the occasion of Urs at Motapir tomb at the village About 16 policemen and six others were injured in the stone-throwing government jeep was also damaged in the incident Those seriously injured were admitted to hospitals at Gandhidham Bhachau and Bhuj police said Meanwhile in Patan town of Mehsana district one person was killed and two injured when some unidentified persons hurled explosive material at them.
