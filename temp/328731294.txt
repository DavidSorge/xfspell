STUDENTS CLASH IN ALIGARH 8 Admitted To Hospital ALIGARH October 2 The District Magistrate of Aligarh in statement to the press today said that following the declaration of results of the Aligarh University Union election last night the students of the Ziauddin Hostel had clashes resulting in injuries to eight students The injured were admitted to the University Hospital To ensure peaceful condition in the city the Vice-Chancellor of the university has given an assurance that the guilty would be severely punished Necessary action is also being taken by the district authorities to bring the guilty to books the press note added -INS
