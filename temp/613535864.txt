Gonda clashes toll 10 LUCKNOW October 2 PTI Six more persons were killed in violent clashes in nearby villages of Colonel Ganj town yesterday taking the death toll in the recent violence in Gonda district to ten an official SDokesman said here One person was killed in Nachli village and four were killed in Pande Choura and Kuthia village reports said Indefinite curfew which was clamped in the town after Sunday's violence which claimed four lives continued for the third day today The deputy inspector general of police Faizabad is camping at CoGanj and the CRPF PAC and police have been deployed at strategic places Clashes occurred when religious procession was stoned fired by some members of another community the spokesman added
