Police fire on Srinagar mob SRINAGAR August 23 UNI Seven people including four policemen were injured when the police opened fire and lobbed tear-gas shells to chase away mob which looted two shops. and set afire house at Kamangarpora in the downtown city today The police said one person Altaf Khandey was seriously injured when he was hit in the chest bullet He has been hospitalized In view of the situation the state authorities extended curfew restrictions in the troubled locality of Kamangarpora Peace was restored this morning in the curfew-bound areas which resulted in the relaxation of curfew restrictions However as tension had been gripping the authorities later decided to reimpose curfew in Nowhatta Hawal and Zadibal areas
