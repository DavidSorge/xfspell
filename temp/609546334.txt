Peaceful bandh in Karnataka towns BANGALORE December 1! UNI The situation in curfew-bound Mysore city was peaceful even as several towns in Karnataka observed bandh today in protest against the publication of short story in Deccan Herald According to official reports reaching here no untoward incident was reported from any part of the state The situation in Bangalore and Tumkur rocked by violence since Sunday was peaceful The publication of the short story had sparked widespread violence leading to police firing in which 16 people were killed Bandh was observed in several towns in Dakshina Kannada Bidar and Gulbarga districts
