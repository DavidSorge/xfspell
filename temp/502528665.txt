Clash Over Census Questionnaire 1 DIES THREE HURT NEAR AMBALA AMBALA February One person was killed and three were injured in clash at Jagad around 40 miles from between two groups of people over the question of the language column in the census questionnaire The Deputy Commissioner of Ambala Mr Sehgal with police reserve guard rushed to Jagadhari to control the situation Five persona have been attested in connection Official sources said that no political party was behind this incident and that the clash was of vate nature The trouble is stated to have arisen when three members of particular community went round the town exhorting the people to enter certain language in the language column of the census quest. The other community also did likewise after some time and wanted the co-religionists to record particular language in the censusPTI
