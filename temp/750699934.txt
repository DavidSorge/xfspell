Curfew clamped on Majalgaon The Times of India New Service AURANGABAD November 23 Curfew has been clamped at Majalgaon in Beed district where the police had to fire 13 rounds yesterday evening to disperse rioting mob killing one person and injuring four The state reserve police have been deployed in the sensitive areas The trouble reportedly started when religious procession reached the old bus stand area and mob collected from the nearby market following some incidents of stone-throwing The police resorted to firing after lathi-charge failed to contain the situation. Some shops were set ablaze
