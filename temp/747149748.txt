Mathura Arrests The Times of India News Service MATHURA August 26 One hundred and eighty-nine persons were arrested here yesterday for defying the curfew which was clamped down on the city fast night. from 10 pm to 5 am this morning All the arrested persons were sentenced to pay varying amounts of fine report circulated by news agency from Agra that 60 huts had been burnt down in the recent disturbances have been officially denied The district authorities stated that only 16 huts were destroyed
