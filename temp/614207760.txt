One killed in Bharuch clash BHARUCH October 18 PTI A driver was killed and two others were injured in stabbing incidents as group clashes broke out in Bharuch town of central Gujarat late last night the police said The driver was fatally stabbed by the rampaging mob in the Chakala area who set the vehicle afire The police burst teargas shells and made lathi-charge as brick-batting by the rival groups persisted in the locality late in the night Two more stabbing incidents and the burning of scooters were reported during the last 24 hours Shops schools and business established the municipal market did not open today.
