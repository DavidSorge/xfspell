One killed in Meerut firing MEERUT July 1 PTI One person was killed and three security forces personnel were injured when the police opened fire to quell mob in the Valley Bazaar area here today The district magistrate Mr Kaushik told newsmen this evening that the police foiled some attempts of arson and looting The mob indulged in stone-throwing and hurled bomb in which PAC deputy superintendent of police and three constables including two PAC jawans were injured The police recovered an unidentified body bearing multiple stab wounds in the Iswarpuri M6halla this afternoon The authorities clamped curfew all over the trouble-torn city from 7 pm this evening to 4 am tomorrow Curfew was however relaxed in the Sadar Bazaar areas for two hours from 5 pm to 7 pm
