EDITORS DEMAND INQUIRY Concern Over Situation The Times of India News Service DELHI February 10 Muslim editors of nine local Urdu newspapers and periodicals in joint statement today demanded inquiry by High Court Judge into the riots in Jabalpur and other areas of Madhya Pradesh They also demanded that the administration in the troubled areas should be handed over to the Army to restore law and order They further demanded that compensation should be paid for the loss of life and property The signatories expressed concern over the riots as authorities have failed to control them and maintain law and order.
