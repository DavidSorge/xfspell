2 more deaths as violence continues in Swat The Times of India News Service SURAT Violence continued for the second day on Friday in the textile city with two more deaths reported taking the toll in the disturbances in the wake of the VHPsponsored Gujarat bandh to five While one person was killed in mob violence another was killed in police firing BJP leader Shyamrao Shrivastav husband of former BJP corporator Shardaben from Dindoli was also killed by mob But the police later clarified that the killing had nothing to do with the current violence. He was killed by group of people belonging to the majority community over property dispute He had received stab injuries and was brought to Mahavir hospital where he succumbed to his injuries As the violence continued on Friday and spread to new areas the authorities deployed Rapid Action Force and additional companies of the State Reserve Police
