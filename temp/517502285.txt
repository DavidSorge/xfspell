Peoples probe into riots By StaffReporter BOMBAY Feb 5 Peoples inquiry at the peoples level into the riots that shook the city in December and January will be conducted by two former high court judges the Indian Peoples Human Rights Commission announced here today The chairman of the Human Rights Tribunal Mr Krishna Iyer former supreme court judge told presspersons that the panel comprising Mr Justice Hosberg Suresh and Mr Justice Daud would submit its report in three months Among other things the panel would consider whether the riots were the result of collusion between communal elements and the administration the role of the much-invoked foreign hand and whether the police were guilty of excesses Mr Iyer said emphasis would be placed or study of the efficacy of the relief rehabilitation package announced by the administration as well as the disbursement of compensation.
