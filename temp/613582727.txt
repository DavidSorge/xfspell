Firing in city By Staff Reporter BOMBAY April 26 THE police opened fire to quell riot tonight between two groups at Musalirkhana stones throw away from the police headquarters near Phule Market Police reinforcements were rushed to the area and Muhammed All Road and all roads leading to it were closed to traffic for two hours till 10 pm Police officers at the scene said they did not know how the clash occurred The road from the Muhammed Ali Road Phule Market to the police headquarters and Road up to the School of Art was littered with ed water bottles used in the clash The Palin Road police said that several persons were injured in the clash though they could not say if anyone was hurt in the firing Picture on Page 9
