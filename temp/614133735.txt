Curfew still in force in Aligarh October 24 PTI Dusk-to-dawn curfew continues to be in force in Aligarh as precautionary measure though the town has been incident-free for the past several days it was officially stated here today Curfew was first imposed in the town on October 5 following riots between two sections of the people resulting in 12 deaths and injury to many others. According to an official spokesman here with normalcy fast returning to the town cinema halls have been permitted to have matinee and evening shows
