2 KILLED AND 11 HURT IN CLASH Curfew In Kotah JAIPUR September 30 Two persons were killed and eleven injured two of them seriously in clashes between two groups in Kotah this afternoon according to report received here tonight The injured included two policemen The report said that the clash occurred when a group of people carrying the image of Lord Ganesh arrived in locality in the city Curfew was immediately clamped down in the city the report added.PTI
