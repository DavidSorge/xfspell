One shot dead in community clash The Times of India News Service CALCUTTA Tension prevailed in the Khardah area of North 24 Parganas on Tuesday after clash involving people of two communities Durga puja pandal in which one person was killed We have stepped up security to ensure peaceful immersion of the idols on Wednesday senior district police official said reporters West Bengal finance minister Asim Dasgupta said that the trouble began when three youths on motorcycles were stopped at Durga puja pandal by some members of the puja committee In the ensuing scuffle one of the youth was shot dead the official said adding that the police had set up picket and were keeping tight vigil till the Kali puja
