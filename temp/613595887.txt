6000 held Ayodhya satyagraha ends AYODHYA January 14 PTI About 6# kar sevaks were arrested here today on the last day of the 40-day satyagraha demanding permission for the construction of the Ram temple The arrested sevaks included those from Britain the US Canada and Hongkong The arrests were led by Jagad Guru Ramanujacharya Swami Sudarshan Acharya and Mahant Purmatma Das Talking to newsmen later Mr Giriraj Kishore VHP joint general secretary said about 300,000 kar sevaks including women had been arrested during the 40-day satyagraha
