Shots fired at police ALLAHABAD August 21 Fall Shots were fired at the police in the Goal Park area here at 930 pm in the sixth such incident today Senior police officers have rushed to the spot along with as armed police contingent The army was giving cover to the police The district authorities have sent an SOS for rushing the BSF to help the police control the situation According to the latest information two companies of the BSF are arriving here tomorrow to help the police They made searches in the riot-affected area and seized an army grenade pistol several bombs and number of knives
