Bhiwandi killings pre-planned By Staff Renorfer BOMBAY May 21 There is reason to believe that the killing of 27 persons on farm at Bhiwandi on Saturday was planned on Friday itself Reporters. photographers going to Bhiwandi on Friday encountered of Soullis who objected to their attempts to photograph them They had in fact organised road blocks outside the town and permitted reporters and photographers to go ahead only after some hesitation On the way back the reporters encountered the same track As their car slowed down one of the groups was heard telling someone near the truck Go to Ansari compound The chief ministers statement that the grass where the bodies were found was not burnt is incorrect The grass has been burnt wherever the bodies lay many of them near the fence. indicating the possibility of their having been killed as they tried to flee
