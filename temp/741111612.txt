ONE DEAD THREE HURT IN FIRING BHUBANESWAR March 22 One man was killed and three were injured when the military opened fire tonight in Rourkela to disperse riotous crowd of 500 in the area according to an official report received here after midnight The military resorted to firing when the crowd did not pay heed to its warning to disperse from the curfewbound area Seven rounds were fired crowd dispersed thereafter PTI
