Part of Indore under curfew INDORE April 6 major portion of the western part of the city was brought under curfew from 530 pm today after an altercation between the driver and youth led to clashes between two groups in the Malharganj area The police separated the groups after lathi-charging tear-gassing and firing in the air The city superintendent of police Mr Surjeet Singh two officers constable and six others were injured in the stone-throwing the police said Section 144 Cr PC has also been enforced in the affected area for seven days As the police were trying to disperse rioters an unknown person fired shot from gun on group in the nearby Luharpattt area
