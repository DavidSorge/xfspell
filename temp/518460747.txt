Curfew imposed in UP town By Our Correspondent June 26 An indefinite curfew has been imposed in Khurja town 16 km from here following clashes between members of two communities last night in which two people were killed The bursting of crackers by marriage party is said to have led to clashes Mr Goswami commissioner of Meerut division and Mr Kaul deputy inspector-general police Meerut range rushed to the spot The district magistrate Mr Atul Chaturvedi and the senior superintendent of police Mr Kanth have also rushed to the town with heavy contingents of the PAC and the armed police The district magistrate told newsmen today that the situation in Khiuja was now under control.
