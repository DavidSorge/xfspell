Gujarat cops seize powerful bombs The Times of India News Service AHMEDABAD August 22 Six crude bombs capable of blowing off houses and killing people within 100 feet were seized by the police Saraspur predominantly labour area in the city Three persons who were waiting to deliver the box were arrested The police also arrested railway porter who was reportedly helping them in delivering the bombs UNI adds from Baroda Barring one case of stabbing in which youth was seriously injured no major incident was reported from the city The incident took place in the curfew-bound Panigard area There was also an attempt to set on fire timber yard in the Wadi area late last night In view of the improving situation curfew will be relaxed tomorrow in Wadi Rapura and Kareli Baug police station limits Only women were allowed to come out to buy milk today but there will be no such restrictions tomorrow
