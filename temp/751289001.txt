16 held as riots rock Dharavi By Staff Reporter BOMBAY October 20 Five people were injured when riots broke out at Dharavi between members of two communities this morning. Sixteen people were arrested according to press note issued by the police The trouble arose when a person threw bucketful of water at man belonging to another community who along with others were trying to extinguish fire that broke out in hut at Azad Nagar colony at Dharavi Soon the incident was blown out of proportion and battle of aerated water bottles ensued People also started throwing stones at each other. Following the tension police combed the entire area for antisocial elements and kept heavy at several sensitive spots.
