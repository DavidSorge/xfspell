Aid for riot-hit The Times of India News Service NEW DELHI May 19 Mr Ra Gandhi Congress general secretary today announced donation of Ki lakhs on behalf of the AICC the families of the victims of the violence in Bhiwandi and others in Maharashtra Mr Siuiram Kesri treasurer to newsmen that the cheque for the amount was being sent to the Man rashtra PCC for onward Mr Chandulal Chandrakar Congress General secretary has flown to Bonbay today to study the situation.
