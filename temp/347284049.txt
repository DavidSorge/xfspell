Shoot at sight order in Ranchi RANCHI August 30 The Government of Bihar has instructions to the army and the police to shoot at sight any person or persons indulging in riotous acts in Ranchi town is officially learnt This decision has been taken to prevent any stray case of stabbing in remote places where anti-social elements may become active The situation in Ranchi and its industrial satellite township of Hatia remained normal for the last 24 hours and no incident was reported this periodPTL
