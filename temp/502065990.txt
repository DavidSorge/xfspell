More troops in Bombay BOMBAY Jan 25 An additional 14 army columns were deployed here today to help the security forces retain their tenacious grip which was rocked by violence early this month With the arrival of more troops by air and road yesterday the city has about 44 army columns apart from the Border Security Force the Rapid Action Force and Central and State Reserve Police Forces which are keeping strict vigil according to PTI Truckloads of army personnel continuing their round-the-clock patrolling in sensitive areas Night curfew clamped after the fresh outbreak of violence is still in force in 25 police station limits.
