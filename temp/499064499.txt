Mob attacks AP police station HYDERABAD March 30 mob of nearly 2000 attacked the Nallakunta police station in the university area here this evening broke open doors and set on fire motorcycle and few. cycles in the compound following the death of a person who was to have been beaten by the police last night Two platoons of armed police fired teargas shells to quell the mob and virtually saved the station and the policemen including sub-inspector locked up inside the station The commissioner of police Mr Munuswamy and other senior officers are holding an inquiry into the incident.
