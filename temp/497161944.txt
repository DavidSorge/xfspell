Hazaribagh limping back to normalcy 1 April 17 This riot-torn town and its neighbouring areas were limping back to normal if no incident of violence reported in the past 24 hours The 24-hour curfew relaxed at noon today for tour hours was reimposed as precautionary measure would remain in force till 7 am tomorrow Several people were rounded up last night The number of persons killed in the past six days was 12 according to official sources-UNI
