Calm in Nadiad Jan 13 UNL The situation in the five curfew-bound areas of this central-Gujarat town which was the scene of violence last Sunday was peaceful with no untoward incident reported today According to police sources the three-hour curfew relaxation this afternoon passed off peacefully call for Nadiad bandh given by the citizens today met with good response with 70 per cent of shops. business establishments and educational institutions remaining closed
