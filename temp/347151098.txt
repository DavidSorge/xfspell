No Strikes Now CALCUTTA January 13 The proposed strike by Calcutta over 7000 tramway workers from Wednesday has been deferred to later date it was announced today The Central Joint Committee of the Tramwaymen said today in press statement that in view of the present tension in the city it was essential to run the tram services regularly to serve the public and to help restore normalcy The Strike call given by the Hindu Mahasabha on Wednesday to protest against the communal disturbances in the two East Pakistan districts of Khulna and Jessore has also been called off
