Probe ordered into Hapur violence The Times of India News Service LUCKNOW Feb 14 The toll in communal violence in Hapur town in Ghaziabad district has gone up to nine The police has arrested persons for abetting violence and for violating curfew According to an official spokesman curfew continued for the second day today in Hapur No untoward incident was reported from any part of the town government meanwhile has announced judicial inquiry into the communal riot in Hapur The government today announced an ex-gratia relief Rs 50000 each to the dependents of the persons killed in the Riots spokesman said the clashes broke out between two communities in Hapur on Wednesday night after the police tried to shift place of worship of one community to place owned by members of another community The police resorted to firing killing four persons on the spot.
