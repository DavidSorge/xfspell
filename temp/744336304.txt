1 killed as cops fired on Baroda mob The Times of India News Service BARODA August 21 One person was killed and two were injured in police firing here today raising the toll to six dead and 50 injured in the last three days of violence here Several congested localities around the centre of the city have been under curfew since the eruption of communal violence on Tuesday night Incidents of stone-throwing and mob attack using acid bulbs which necessitated the police firing this afternoon led to cancellation of the curfew relaxation in the Wadi police station area. According to the police commissioner Mr Mehta curfew was relaxed peacefully in areas under the Rapura Karelibaug and city police stations He said the police had fired two rounds at building in Bakripole in Ladwada locality in the verge of group clash
