14 hurt in clash in XJI township June 16 PTI Fourteen people were injured today in violent clash between two groups belonging to different communities in Haiderghart township of Barabanki district about 50 km from here according to an official report Senior police officials have rushed from here to the township where heavy police and PAC patrolling -is now going on to control the situation Two groups clashed following dispute over the construction of place of worship Sharp-edged weapons were also used in the clash. Eighty people have been taken into custody so far.
