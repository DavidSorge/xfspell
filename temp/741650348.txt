Mob violence arson near Ahmednagar AHMEDNAGAR July 30 PTI Normalcy was restored Puntamba township about 10 km north of here after flare-up of mob fury and arson yesterday evening, the police said. trouble arose over alleged attempts yesterday afternoon at removing an idol on the banks of the Godavari near the township group of angry residents collected and started throwing stones in the police said motor-cycle and liquor shop were set ablaze later by mob of 500 which also ransacked the guest house of place of worship in the township the district superintendent of police Mr Dhananjay Jadhav told PTI today.
