Tension prevails in Baroda By Our Staff Correspondent BARODA December 9 Tension prevailed in the walled areas today which were put under curfew last night following incidents of stone-throwing The Police took into custody late last night Dr Jaffar Bandukwala reader in the science faculty of University under section 151 of the Bombay Police Act Dr Bandukwala heading young Muslim action committee which had been organising the police commissioners office to protest against the alleged police atrocities during the riots last month Over 20 preventive arrests were made today it was learnt Even though curfew was lifted at 10 am in some areas barring the Golwad Bhadra Kacheri Navapur and Branpura pockets most of the shops remained closed In Panigate the relaxation had to be withdrawn at 230. pm following stone-throwing incidents
