Army chief visits medical camp By Staff Reporter BOMBAY December 16 The chief of army staff General Rodrigues visited medical camp set up by the army for the riotaffected people at Bharat Nagar Bandra east yesterday Over 1200 patients suffering from various ailments who were unable to go to hospitals on account of curfew and fear were treated by army doctors at the camp An equal number of patients were treated in two other army medical camps at Dongri and Defense patients who were injured in the riots were also treated The army chief expressed satisfaction over the medical facilities being provided at these camps.
