Riot-torn areas in UP peaceful The Times of India News Service MUZAFFARNAGAR October 13 Stray incidents of arson and brickbatting were reported from the walled city despite curfew It was relaxed for two hours in Khatauli from 2 pm to 4 pm when no untoward incident was reported Sayyed Ahmad Bukhari son by Imam Bukhari and leader of the Adam Sena who was coming here from Delhi was prevented from entering the district.
