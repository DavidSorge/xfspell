Houses set ablaze at Kopargaon September 21 UNI Tension prevailed at Kopargaon in Ahmednagar district as an unruly mob went on violent spree setting fire to some 6hop houses and place of worship According to the police The police resorted to lathi-charge to control the violent mob which also indulged in stone-throwing Over 150 persons were injured the police told UNI They said about 120. persons have so far been arrested The district collector Mr Anil Kumar Lakhina and senior police officials are camping in the city Meanwhile ban on the assembly of persons and carrying of arms ammunition was imposed on the city till tomorrow.
