Riots planned says Union minister PATNA April 16 PTI The Union minister of state for planning Mr Fazlur Rahman said today that the disturbances at Jamshedpur were engineered and planned talking to newsmen here after two-day tour of the riot-torn steel city Mr Rahman said that the main cause of its provocation which led to the widespread disturbances was the detention of the Ramnavami procession on the first day near Mango police station Janata legislator who was leading the procession declared that the procession would not move unless all the persons arrested earlier as precautionary measure were released Mr Rahman said When asked whether Janata partymen opposed to Mr Karpoori Thakur chief minister had hand in the riots Mr Rahman said that it could not be ruled out as the Janata party. unit at Jamshedpur was divided into three or four factions
