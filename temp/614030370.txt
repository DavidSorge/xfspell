Mob tear-gassed in Kashmir town SRJINAGAR July 13 The police used tear-gas to disperse violent mob at the ancient temple town of Mattan about 60 km from here yesterday an official report received here Today said there had been tension in the town following dispute over common path leading to two different places of worship The use of tear-gas followed acts of violence in which the windscreen of the car of the deputy commissioner of Anantnag district had been smashed the same
