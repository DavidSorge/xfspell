1 dies in fresh Raroda violence BARODA December 1 UNI Three people were assaulted one of them fatally in renewed outbreak of violence here today forcing authorities to impose curfews in one police station area While two people were stabbed in the Nawa Bazar area an autorickshaw was set on fire near Champaner Barwaza in the old city One of the stab victims 32 year-old man was declared dead on being brought to the Shri Sayyaji General SSG hospital in the city this morning sources at the hospital said The autorickshaw driver who sustained razor injury was also admitted to the SSG hospital Indefinite curfew was imposed in the city police station area of the walled city at II am to contain the violence, the police said
