Most shops open in Aurangabad AURANGABAD June 12 Most shops Opened today and people went about their work as the situation returned normal in the town after the recent disturbances However the curfew which had been lifted early in the morning was as precautionary measure at 7 pm The curfew is to end at 5 pm tomorrow Sixteen people were arrested for various offences including arson and looting during the disturbances The police have arrested 27 for violation of curfew and Section 144 PTI
