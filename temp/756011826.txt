Ramlila procession in Delhi again NEW DELHI October 6 PTI The Ramlila procession after being suspended for two days today passed through the old route in the walled city amidst tight security. arrangements The procession was taken out following withdrawal of cases against all the 41 people arrested after Saturday's violence over the decision to change the traditional route The deputy commissioner of police north district Ms Kiran Bedi who supervised the security arrangements as the procession passed through the troubled areas without any untoward incident said all the arrested had been released Meanwhile all shops and business establishments in north Delhi remained closed today in response to bandh called by the Bharatiya Janata Party and the Vishwa Hindu Parishad
