Indore officials transfer aborted BHOPAL Oct 21 The Motilal Vora government has backtracked on the decision to transfer the Indore divisional commissioner Mr Sood According to official sources Mr Vora decided to make overall changes in the administrative and police set-up at Indore so that the confidence of the people who were badly shaken by communal riots could be restored he had ordered that the commissioner the inspector-general of police the collector and the superintendent of police be all transferred out of Indore
