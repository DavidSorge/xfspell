Boy dies in firing in Ahmedabad AHMEDABAD January 14 UNI PTI 12-year-old boy was killed and young man injured when the police opened fire communal clashes in the citys Dariapur and Kalupur areas today The trouble arose over kite-flying on Makar Sankaranti today at Nagina Pole and Wadgam in the Dariapur area The police fired 23 rounds at two places after bursting number of teargas shells in vain attempt to control the mob throwing stones and burning cloth pieces at one another Eleven police personnel including three officers and of rioters were injured in stone-throwing The boy succumbed to bullet injuries in the Hospital while the man had been admitted to the civil hospital where he was stated to be out of danger
