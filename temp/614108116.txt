Four killed in Allahabad riot ALLAHABAD June 15 PTI UNI Four persons were killed and 16 injured following clashes between two groups of people in part of the thickly populated locality of the city An indefinite curfew was clamped in the areas under five police stations of the city The superintendent of police Mr Bajpai said the trouble started last night following an altercation between members of two communities over some shops near place of worship at Patthargali under the Shahganj police station Rampaging mobs indulged in firing arson and stabbing
