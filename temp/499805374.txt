Relief for riot-hit Jogeshwari families By Staff Reporter Cash relief was given to 287 riot-affected families in Jogeshwari by the minister of state for labour and education Mr Ramnath Pande in Bombay on Friday Each family gets cash relief ranging from Rs 100 to Rs 500 depending upon the magnitude of their losses The relatives of the people who died in mob fury were granted Rs 2000 per head About 80 homeless families were shifted to Malwani
