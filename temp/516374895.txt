Stern action against killings urged BvA Staff Reporter BOMBAY January 16 The executive committee of the Bar Association of India at its meeting held in New Delhi yesterday viewed with alarm and despair the complete lack of will by those responsible for maintaining law and order to prevent and stop the wilful killing of innocent people the forcible entry of miscreants into homes wanton destruction of property in Bombay and Ahmedabad The association appealed to the government at the Centre and the states to meet the ugly situation with firm hand.
