Outsiders behind Agra riots says UP official By Our Correspondent AGRA May 6 Mr Shyam Singh Bisen home secretary of Uttar Pradesh told newsmen yesterday that outside elements were responsible for fomenting trouble in Agra which kept widespread arson looting and rioting Mr Bisen was however not able to place before the press any solid evidence to show the involvement of outside elements He said the trouble started almost simultaneously in the Jatavlocalities and this alone showed that peace in the city was disturbed in pre-planned and calculated manner efforts were being made to trace those responsible for fomenting trouble that culminated in the death of eight people in police firings besides large-scale arson and looting.
