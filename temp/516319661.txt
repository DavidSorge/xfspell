Youths protest against Aligarh atrocities By Our Staff Correspondent NEW DELHI November 25 About 1000 Muslim youths yesterday took out silent procession to protest against atrocities on the minority. community in Aligarh memorandum submitted to the Prime Minister demanded an end to these atrocities and punishment to those responsible for the recent killing of several persons in Aligarh The memorandum copies of which were also submitted to the speaker of the Lok Sabha and the chairman of Rajya Sabha also demanded disbanding of the provincial armed lawyer and creation of special police force with adequate representation to the minority community An immediate ban on the holding of the RSS in the premises of educational institutions and adequate compensation to the dependents of those killed or injured were among the other demands.
