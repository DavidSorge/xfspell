Police open lire on rioting mob in Beed By Staff Reporter April 19 The police yesterday fired seven rounds from revolvers to quell rioting mob in Mazalgaon town in Beed district Twenty persons. including eight policemen were injured in stone throwing incidents which were sparked after unruly mob set fire to shop and attacked procession at around 80 pm the state police control said here today Eighteen people were arrested on charges of rioting The situation was now under control spokesman of the state police said.
