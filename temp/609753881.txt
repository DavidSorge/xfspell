Bomb attack on Kalyan denied February 16 The situation in Hapur remained calm today and curfew was relaxed for hours while authoritative sources denied an earlier report of bomb attack on the UP chief minister Mr Kalyan Singh when he visited the riot-hit areas last night reports PTI The entire city has been put under PAC forces and watch towers set up for round-the-clock vigil The disputed temple site has been put under independent charge of the district magistrate to maintain status quo under Ghaziabad court order In Lucknow the secretary to the chief minister Mr Nipen Mishra denied that bombs were thrown at the chief minister He said no such incidents had taken place at any stage of Mr Singh's visit
