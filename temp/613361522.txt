1 killed in Surat unrest The Times of India News Service SURAT Mohammed Madni Abdul Karim Cutpiecewallah 20 was killed when police fired in the trouble-torn Bhaga Talav area here on Thursday morning According to the Cutpiecewallahs the youth who was injured in police firing was not allowed by one duty personnel to be taken to hospital for treatment It was only after much pleading that his father was allowed to take his injured son to the Mulla Lokhart Hospital where he succumbed to his injuries Later his body was taken to the Surat Civil Hospital for postmortem examination The situation in Bhaga Talav continued to remain tense.
