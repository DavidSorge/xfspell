COW SLAUGHTER CAUSES RIOT 12 Persons Injured The Times of India News Service MEETUP January 8 About dozen persons were injured and property worth Rs 5000 was looted in riot at Ghaziabad on January 6 according to communique issued by the District Magistrate Meerut this morning The riot was sequel to the slaughter of stolen cow Twenty-four persons including six alleged to be responsible for the slaughter were arrested the press note added The situation at Ghaziabad is now reported to be normal
