Arms seized Rhiwandi THANE June 28 PTI stockpile of lethal weapons six country-made four country-made pistols live cartridges and pellets have been seized in Bhiwaudi town of Thane district police said here today The seizure followed concerted drive launched by the police recently under the supervision of Mr Gaikwad DCP and Mr Akkar ACP Bhiwandi The police also seized some machinery and tools and other articles used for missiles Fifteen persons have been arrested in this connection the police say Night curfew was reimposed at Kherwadi in northwest Bombay which witnessed fresh spell of riots since the last eight days the police said today.
