Shops open in Jamshedpur JAMSHEDPUR September 3 UNI All was quiet in the riot-torn steel city today All shops and establishments opened normally except in the Jugsalairea where section of people shouting slogans condemning atrocities on them in the name of secularism forced the closure of shops Vehicular traffic was also normal today With some more arrests last night the total number of persons rounded up in connection with the fresh flare-up rose to 601 according to official sources Paddy Meanwhile peace committee meeting was held at the Tatanagar railway station today to restore confidence among the railway staff according to the station superintendent
